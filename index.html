Funding

Hosting Events

  • Complete event proposal 15 days before the proposed date
  • Submit budget, room or table booking request along with the proposal
  • Must be approved by SSF
  • Better do poll first, estimate the number of attendees before estimating the budget
  • If the budget changes, must inform the treasurer, who then will inform SSF

Meetings

  • Biweekly or as neccessary
  • Notes will be taken during the meeting
  • Summary will be sent out after meeting
  • Find a time that works for everyone
